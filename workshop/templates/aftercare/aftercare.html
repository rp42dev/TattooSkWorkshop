{% translate 'Which cream should I use?' %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %}We recommend to use Bepanthen Sensi Calm cream (not salve). Its not to sticky to the clothes. But if you don’t have it - salve also is good. Or you can use the cream from our studio. Nothing more is acceptable.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate 'How to take care of my fresh tattoo?' %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %}You keep the plastic film for 2 days. After 2 days remove the plastic film, wash tattoo well with cool water and soap (soap with no colour no perfume). Let the tattoo dry itself or gently blot it with a towel. Put the cream on, with very thin layer. Repeat that combination 3-4 times a day. After one week from plastic film removed u can use the cream 1-2 times a day. When you see the tattoo is dry, put cream on. NECESSARY: to moisturise tattoo to avoid crusts.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate 'If under the plastic film is a lot of liquid?' %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %}If you see plastic film falling off (the tattoo is not covered with film) or its a lot of liquid under, just remove it. Don’t need to wait 5 days. Remove the plastic film, wash your tattoo and use the cream.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate 'Can I do training?' %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %}We not recommend to train at all during the plastic film is on. During the training tattooed place will sweat too and you will get a lot of bubbles, which means plastic film has to be removed. It will cause burning feeling during the process and tattoo can be damaged.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate 'Can I go to the shower?' %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %}Yes, you can do all your routine as normal.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate 'Can I go to solarium (take a sun) or sauna?' %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %}Sauna is strictly forbidden! Till recovery process is finished. Solarium is not aloud for two weeks, later you must cover your tattoo with some cotton material till the recovery process finished. Further use the sun protection minimum plus 50 SPF for whole your life (same on the sun).{% endblocktranslate %}
